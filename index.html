About Newtown Surveying

Independent cost advice that protects project value

Newtown Surveying supports clients from feasibility through to final account, helping teams make informed decisions at every stage of construction delivery.

Our practice combines market intelligence, robust measurement, and practical contract guidance to maintain budget certainty without compromising quality.

Core Services

Commercial clarity from concept to completion

Cost Planning

Elemental cost plans, benchmark analysis, and option studies to establish realistic budgets from early design stages.

Procurement Advice

Tender documentation, contractor comparison, and procurement strategy tailored to programme, risk, and value objectives.

Contract Administration

Interim valuations, change control, claims evaluation, and final account negotiation under standard and bespoke contracts.

Our Process

A structured approach to budget confidence

  1. 01

    Project Brief Review

    We assess scope, quality benchmarks, and constraints to define the commercial baseline for the scheme.

  2. 02

    Cost Strategy

    A transparent cost model and risk register are prepared to guide decisions before procurement.

  3. 03

    Tender & Award

    We manage tender clarifications and evaluations to secure suitable contractors and robust contract terms.

  4. 04

    Delivery & Final Account

    Ongoing cost reporting, valuation checks, and final account close-out keep financial outcomes aligned with plan.
